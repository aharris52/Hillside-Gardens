About

Since 1983 Hillside Gardens Sawdust & Shavings has been the choice for your wood products needs. Whether it's a small landscape fresh-up, or a complete transformation we have the product you need at a fair price. Recognized by the Pierce County Conservation District as an outstanding community partner. We're here to help you conserve and beautify your own small piece of the Great Northwest.



Products

We carry the finest locally-sourced, all natural wood products. You won't find any artificial dyes or petro-chemical fertilizers here. We sell only nature's best and untreated products. All top soil is organic and locally sourced, and the bark and chips are from local suppliers.


Services

We have been in business over 30 years because we know how business is done. If you need delivery to your home, jobsite, or farm we're here for you.
